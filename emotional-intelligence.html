What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to understand behaviours of others and controlling own emotions.


How is it different to IQ?

EQ is more emption and empathy based while IQ is academic and a way to identify people with exceptional intelligence. Also, EQ can be taught more so than IQ can be.


Why is it important?

It means you are able manage your emotions better and have a better understands of others. This can then result in having better relationships with others, a more enjoyable work environment and excel in leadership roles.
