Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why

I worked in a team of a 12, were we had about 17 different tasks we completed. I was trained in nearly all tasks while some others only had training in one or two tasks. This put a lot of pressure on other members of the team. When I approached the Team Leaderr about this, it was meet with hostile. The next time I brought it up, I presented it more like this is how the team feels when we are under pressure and what can we do about it? "Maybe we should be training others to take on new tasks". This approach was more warmly welcomed. Rather than say “This person needs to step up...” presenting the issue that the team has and asking about a solution was a much better approach.
